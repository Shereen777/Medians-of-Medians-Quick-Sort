What's happening

Ready to sort. Press Start or Step to begin.

Median of Medians picks a reliably balanced pivot in linear time: split the range into groups of 5, find each group's median, then recursively find the median of those medians. The result is used as a normal Quick Sort pivot, so partitioning still proceeds exactly like the classic algorithm.
